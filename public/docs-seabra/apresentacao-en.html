The problem your operation faces today

  • Notebooks and spreadsheets: notes get lost, data goes stale, animal history becomes fragmented.
  • Overloaded technicians: farm visits without structured records, evaluations that never reach the producer.
  • Guesswork-driven decisions: with no production, reproduction or health indicators, the farm operates in the dark.
  • Associations blind to their herd: each producer works in isolation, with no aggregated data to negotiate, research or prove value to the community.
  • Paper in the field: rain, dust, loss, rework — and zero traceability.

The solution in 4 pillars

Seabra Small Ruminants organizes everything your operation needs across four integrated fronts:

01 · OPERATIONS

Farm routine

Herd, events (birth, sale, death, abortion, culling), daily production, milk recording, lactations, unlimited staff with role-based permissions, in-app and WhatsApp notifications, financials.

02 · TECHNICAL

Field work for technicians

Dairy LTE (Linear Type Evaluation, 16 traits) and Meat LTE (13 traits), body measurements, pregnancy diagnosis, CMT, FAMACHA, body condition score — all working offline.

Association management

Supported segments

The system adapts to your production profile. Screens, indicators and flows change by segment — each producer sees only what's relevant to their reality.

Dairy Goats

  • Milk recording (both milkings)
  • Lactations and drying-off
  • Analyses: SCC, TBC, fat, protein, lactose
  • CMT (California Mastitis Test)
  • Dairy LTE and Pregnancy Diagnosis

Dairy Sheep

  • Milk recording adapted to the species
  • Lactations and milk composition
  • Reproductive and productive indicators
  • LTE and PD with sheep protocol
  • Pre-loaded sheep breeds

Meat Goats

  • Weighings and average daily gain (ADG)
  • Body measurements (heart girth, scrotal circumference)
  • Meat LTE module (13 traits across 4 groups)
  • SMI (Sire Merit Index) and KWW (Kilograms of Weaned Kid)
  • Genealogy and progeny tracking
  • Lot and sector management

Meat Sheep

  • Weight performance per animal and lot (ADG)
  • Body measurements and scores
  • Meat LTE for sheep (13 traits)
  • SMI and KWW for ram selection
  • Reproduction, growing and full traceability to slaughter

Features by user role

Producer

Records what happens on the farm without overcomplicating the daily routine.
  • Herd: registration, editing, filtered listing and detailed individual profile per animal.
  • Events: births, reproduction, abortions, deaths, sales, culling, drying-off, weighing, clinical care and handling.
  • Production: daily production, milk recording, milk output, storage (tank/chiller).
  • Lactations: full history and details of closed lactations.
  • Milk analyses: composition, SCC, TBC, fat and protein.
  • Dashboard: charts of production, herd composition and reproductive indicators.
  • Technical requests: open and track technician visits.
  • Unlimited staff with granular permissions: add as many workers as needed and define exactly what each one can see and do.
  • In-app + WhatsApp notifications: births, PD, weanings, analyses, visits and handling delivered automatically to your phone.
  • Financials: expenses by category, sector and period.
  • CSV export of all farm data at any time.

Technician

Records evaluations and services in the field, often with no signal.
  • Linear Type Evaluation (LTE): two dedicated modules — Dairy LTE (16 traits, focused on udder, teats and dairy conformation) and Meat LTE (13 traits across 4 groups: body structure, meat structure, front limbs and rear limbs). Separate forms for males and females, total scoring and editable history.
  • Body measurements: heart girth, scrotal circumference and objective body metrics.
  • Pregnancy Diagnosis (PD): records with gestational days and identification of reproductive pathologies.
  • Visit management: schedule, farms covered, activity dashboard.
  • Requests: receive, approve and decline producer requests.
  • Full offline mode: everything works with no signal and syncs later.

Association Admin

Sees the aggregated herd of the association and manages technicians and producers.
  • User management: registration and editing of technicians and producers tied to the association.
  • Properties: visualization and usage monitoring of every member farm.
  • Managerial analytics: global metrics, sex distribution, per-farm breakdown, aggregated production.
  • History: advanced filters for records, movements and reproductive events by category.
  • Centralized export: dedicated panel with date filters, per-producer aggregation, 12 data types.
  • Payments: subscriptions, history, delinquency.

Technical differentiators

01 · Offline First

Field work that doesn't depend on a signal

Local storage on every device with automatic syncing and conflict resolution when connectivity returns. The technician can visit the most remote farm and record LTE, measurements and PD through the same familiar workflow.

02 · AI Assistant

An assistant inside the app, always at hand

Answers natural-language questions about your herd based on the actual farm data — production, reproduction, milk quality, health, performance, genetics and much more. The more you record, the more valuable the answers become.

Detects scientific questions (in multiple languages) and automatically searches academic literature. Knows the app manual and answers operational questions adapted to the user role (producer, technician, admin, staff).

03 · CSV data export

Your data available for any external analysis

Producers export their own data from every list screen. The association admin has a centralized panel with date filters and per-producer aggregation. Exports 12 data types: milk recording, handling, weighing, drying-off, birth, sale, death, abortion, culling, clinical, milk output, daily production. UTF-8 CSV files ready for Excel, scientific research or ERP.

04 · Per-farm configuration

The system adjusts to your productive reality

Each farm configures its own parameters: ideal weaning weight and age, age and weight for first breeding, days for pregnancy diagnosis, default birth weight, auto-categorization. For associations and institutions with specific needs, we offer custom development on demand (see commercial models below).

05 · Smart notifications + WhatsApp

The system reminds you before you need to remember

An in-app notification center alerts you to important routine events: expected births, pregnancy diagnoses coming due, upcoming weanings, milk analysis results, technical visit requests, vaccinations and pending handlings. With one click the same notifications are sent directly to the WhatsApp of the producer, technician or worker — no need for push, email or even opening the app.

Births and PD Weaning and weighings Milk analyses Technical visits Handling and vaccines
06 · Unlimited staff with permissions

Your whole farm team using the system

Add as many workers as you want — milkers, caretakers, admin, foreman — with no per-user fee. For each worker the producer defines exactly what they can see and do: access only to milk recording, only to clinical handling, only to reproductive events, or any combination. Everyone does their part without exposing sensitive data or interfering with other workflows.

07 · Multi-tenant and Multi-platform

One platform, many associations, all devices

Each association has its own isolated data and its own admin. The same infrastructure serves many organizations with total separation. Available on Android, iOS and Web, on a single shared database.

Answer: the app was designed for people who wear boots and work with animals. Simple interface, large taps, offline. In-person training available.
